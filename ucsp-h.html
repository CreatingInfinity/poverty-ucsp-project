What is Poverty?

Poverty is defined as a lack of sufficient funds to meet basic needs such as food, clothing, and shelter. However, poverty is much more than simply not having enough money.

Effects of Poverty:

There are numerous reasons for poverty, and neither a single cause nor an all-encompassing cure exists. In reality, the majority of the time, a number of variables combine to generate poverty. Understanding these components in depth and how they work together is the key to eradicating poverty.
